CS Degree Programs

Preparing students for a world that is changing quickly is our primary educational objective. Undergraduates can choose to declare Computer Science as a Minor or as a Bachelor of Science. A Master's or PhD in computer science is an option available to graduate students. A master's degree is a professional degree that is terminal. Those who want to work in research or education should pursue a PhD.
